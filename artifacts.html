This page provides a list of the FHIR artifacts defined as part of this implementation guide.

Knowledge Artifacts: Activity Definitions

These define activities that can be performed as part of content in this implementation guide.

Daily Assessment of the Duration of Antibiotic Therapy

All Antibiotics are daily re-evaluated for the Duration of Therapy for each Antibiotic.
Daily Assessment of the Focus for Antibiotics

All Antibiotics are daily re-evaluated for the Focus for each Antibiotic.
Daily Assessment of the Indication for Antibiotics

All Antibiotics are daily re-evaluated for the Indication for each Antibiotic.
Daily Assessments of Readiness-to-wean from mechanical ventilation

Readiness-to-wean from mechanical ventilation is assessed periodically every day.
Daily Mobilization

Mobilization that occurs at least once a day.
Daily Stop-Orders for Invasive Devices

All invasive devices have a daily re-evaluation regarding the necessity of each device.
Daily Treatment Goals

Daily Treatment Goals are set at least once every day.
Daily Weaning Trials

Weaning trials are performed periodically every day.
Daily multiprofessional ward rounds

Every Intensive Care Patient is visited daily in multiprofessional ward rounds led by an intensive care specialist.
Delirium Assessment during Early Shift

Delirium should be assessed once between 6 AM and <2 PM every day.
Delirium Assessment during Late Shift

Delirium should be assessed once between 2 AM and <10 PM every day.
Delirium Assessment during Night Shift

Delirium should be assessed once between 10 PM and <6 AM every day.
First Communication with Patients and/or their Relatives within 72 hours

An initial structured communication with the Patients and/or their Relatives occurs within 72 hours of Admission to Intensive Care.
First Enteral Feeding Within 24 hours

First Enteral Feeding occurs within 24 hours after Admission to Intensive Care.
First Mobilization within 24 hours

First Mobilization occurs within 24 hours after Admission to Intensive Care.
Frequent Blood Cultures

The Frequency of Blood Cultures is at least 80 per 1000 days.
No Discharge with Home Ventilation

The patient is not discharged with home ventilation.
Pain Assessment during Early Shift

Pain should be assessed once between 6 AM and <2 PM every day.
Pain Assessment during Late Shift

Pain should be assessed once between 2 AM and <10 PM every day.
Pain Assessment during Night Shift

Pain should be assessed once between 10 PM and <6 AM every day.
Sedation Assessment during Early Shift

Sedation should be assessed once between 6 AM and <2 PM every day.
Sedation Assessment during Late Shift

Sedation should be assessed once between 2 AM and <10 PM every day.
Sedation Assessment during Night Shift

Sedation should be assessed once between 10 PM and <6 AM every day.
The daily calorie intake is sufficient

The daily amount of calories matches the individual requirements.
Weekly Communication with Patients and Relatives

At least one structured communication with the Patients and/or their Relatives takes place every week.

Daily Documentation of Treatment Goals

Treatment Goals should be documented daily for every Intensive Care Patient.
Daily Evaluation of Readiness-to-Wean and/or Weaning Trials

All invasive ventilated Intensive Care Patients should be evaluated for their readiness-to-wean and/or receive weaning trials at least once every day.
Daily multiprofessional ward rounds

Every Intensive Care Patient should be visited daily in multiprofessional ward rounds led by an intensive care specialist.
Discharge with dependence on home ventilation

The fraction of patients discharged with dependence on home ventilation should be low.
Driving Pressure Limitation

Driving Pressure should be limited to a maximum of 15 cmH2O in all invasive ventilated ICU patients with severe ARDS.
Early Enteral Feeding

All Intensive Care Patients for which a sufficient oral nutrition is not expected on day one should be started on enteral feeding within 24 hours.
Frequency of Blood Cultures

The Frequency of Blood Cultures should be at least 80 per 1000 days.
Hand Disinfectant Consumption

The consumption of hand disinfectant should be more than 80 litres per 1000 days.
Indication, Focus and Duration of Therapy for Antibiotics

All Intensive Care Patients with Antibiotics should have a daily re-evaluation of the Indication, Focus and Duration of Therapy for each Antibiotic.
Infection-per-Device Ratio

The number of infections per invasive devices is at zero.
Inital Mobilization

All Intensive Care Patients should be mobilized within 24 hours after admission to ICU.
Inital Structured Communication with Patients and/or Relatives

For all Intensive Care Patients, an initial structured communication with the Patients and/or their Relatives should take place within 72 hours of Admission to Intensive Care.

Periodic Assessment of Sedation Level, Analgesia Level and Delirium Status

Sedation Level, Analgesia Level and Delirium Status should be assessed at least once in each shift (time-slots 6-14, 14-22, 22-6) in all Intensive Care Patients.
Periodic Mobilization

All Intensive Care Patients that don’t have a no-mobilization order should be mobilized at least once every day.
Periodic Structured Communication with Patients and/or Relatives

For all Intensive Care Patients, at least one structured communication with the Patients and/or their Relatives should take place every week.
Plateau Pressure Limitation

Plateau Pressure should be limited to a maximum of 30 cmH2O in all invasive ventilated ICU patients with severe ARDS.
Pneumonia-per-Ventilated-Patients Ratio

The number of pneumonia cases per ventilated patients should be low.
Sedation Level, Analgesia Level and Delirium Status in acceptable ranges.

Sedation Level, Analgesia Level and Delirium Status should be in acceptable ranges.
Stop Orders for Invasive Devices

All Intensive Care Patients with invasive devices should have a daily re-evaluation regarding the necessity of each device.
Sufficient Feeding in Non-Adipose Patients

Every day, all Non-Adipose Intensive Care Patients should receive the amount of Calories that match their daily individual requirements.
Tidal Volume Limitation

Tidal volume should be limited to a maximum of 6 ml/kg ideal body weight in all invasive ventilated ICU patients with severe ARDS.
